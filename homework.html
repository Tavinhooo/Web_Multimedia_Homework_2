Here are some multimedia elements:

Photos

JPEG (Lossy Compression): JPEG is a commonly used format for digital photos. It uses lossy compression to reduce file size while maintaining acceptable image quality.

PNG (Lossless Compression): PNG supports transparency and lossless compression, making it ideal for graphics and images requiring high detail.

HDR (High Dynamic Range): HDR images contain a greater range of brightness and contrast, preserving details in both dark and bright areas.

Graphics

SVG (Scalable Vector Graphics): SVG files use vector-based graphics, meaning they can be scaled without losing quality.

GIF (Animated): GIFs support simple animations and transparency but are limited in color depth.
